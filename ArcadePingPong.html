Pong - El Clásico Juego Arcade

Pong es un clásico juego de acrcade creado por Atari en 1972. El objetivo del juego es usar las paletas para golpear la pelota y evitar que anoten en tu lado

Pong has influenced the conception of future video games, such as the popular Atari racing game, Gran Trak 10, which was released in 1974. Pong was also one of the first games to be played with a joystick, a feature that was adopted by many other video games. In the early 1980s, several versions of Pong were released, such as the tabletop Pong game, the board game Pong, and the electronic version of Pong.
